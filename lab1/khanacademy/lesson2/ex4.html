Travel to Hawaii

Hawaiʻi Welcomes You!

Hawaii is a beautiful state made up of 7 inhabited islands , 4 of which are the most popular destinations: Hawaii (The Big Island), Maui, Oahu, and Kauai. To plan your trip, consider the dates that work for you and whether you prefer to go during the dry or wet season. Choosing the island (or islands) you want to go to will also help you draft a budget for yourself and your fellow travelers. Flying isn’t cheap, so booking your flight and accommodations early is usually the best choice. Checking the weather a day or two before you leave will help you pack appropriate clothing and gear. Once you’re in Hawaii, kick back and enjoy the stunning natural beauty and friendly “Aloha Spirit!”

In short, the best options for your trip to Hawaii are:

  • Choose an island and spend a week there.
  • Make a combination of two or three islands (Oahu and Maui, or Kauai and Maui).

Nature:

If you want to optimize your time and make the most of your vacation in Hawaii, here are the best excursions and tours you should do on your trip to the Hawaiian archipelago:

  • Pearl Harbor and Oahu: Full-Day Oahu Essentials Tour
  • Dolphin watching and snorkeling excursion in Oahu
  • Hawaii Big Island Tour: A full day excursion flying from Oahu.
  • Sunrise tour at the top of the volcano in Maui, with the best views of the island.
  • Snorkeling excursion in the Molokini crater on the island of Maui.
  • Hana Highway Tour, panoramic tour on the island of Maui full day.
  • Lanai: half-day Hawaiian diving adventure, for lovers of marine life.
  • Half-day Hawaii Manoa Waterfall Hiking Tour.
  • Kaua’i: 4-hour tour of the spectacular Waimea Canyon.

Views:

Hawaii is a land of great diversity, beauty, and many contradictions. The permeating spirit of Aloha, the rich culture of the people, and the sacred and historical traditions passed down for generations continue to define these isolated islands. Mother Nature pulled out all the stops when forming this incredible place. From lush rainforests to barren deserts, Hawaii is as eclectic a place as you'll ever visit. A favorite getaway for people worldwide, Hawai'i has become famous for its miles of beaches, erupting volcanoes, lush rainforests, exquisite food, and exotic flora and fauna.
