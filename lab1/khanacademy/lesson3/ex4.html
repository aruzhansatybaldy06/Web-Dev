Recipe 1: Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients
Quantity Ingredient
500g Spaghetti
400g Ground Beef

Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 30 minutes
  • Servings: 4

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, brown ground beef over medium heat.
