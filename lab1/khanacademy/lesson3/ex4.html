Recipe 1: Delicious Pancakes

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup

Details: These pancakes are light, fluffy, and perfect for breakfast or brunch. Enjoy them with your favorite toppings!

  • Step 1: Mix dry ingredients.
  • Step 2: Add wet ingredients and stir until just combined.
  • Step 3: Cook on a hot griddle until golden brown on both sides.

Recipe 2: Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredient Quantity
Ground beef 1 lb
Tomato sauce 2 cups

Details: A hearty and flavorful pasta dish that's a family favorite. Perfect for a cozy dinner!

  • Step 1: Brown the ground beef in a pan.
  • Step 2: Add tomato sauce and simmer for 20 minutes.
  • Step 3: Serve over cooked spaghetti.

Recipe 3: Refreshing Fruit Salad

Ingredient Quantity
Watermelon 2 cups, cubed
Strawberries 1 cup, sliced

Details: A light and healthy fruit salad that's perfect for a summer day or as a side dish at any gathering.

  • Step 1: Combine all the fruits in a large bowl.
  • Step 2: Toss gently and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  • Step 3: Serve chilled.
